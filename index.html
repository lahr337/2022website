  • OUR VISION

"To rescue abandoned, abused, neglected, and unwanted huskies. Rehabilitate and find qualified loving homes for them throughout Louisiana and beyond. To educate the community about responsible pet ownership and the special qualities, needs and traits of the Siberian Husky Breed."

Our goal is for Louisiana Husky Rescue to be the beacon for potential husky adopters to turn to when looking for their next companion; while ensuring that all treatable rescued huskies find loving homes.

Louisiana Husky Rescue is a passionate family prioritizing the husky breed, educating the public about them and ensure that rescues are valued and treated with respect, guaranteeing they are placed in deserving homes.
